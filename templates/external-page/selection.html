Cricket


Process for Selection in Cricket Team


1.  Join Cricket Academy or Local Club
You get yourself enrolled in a local club or cricket academy where you start learning the tricks of the trade. If the authorities like you as a player, they draft you into their team which plays at various levels and a good performance can elevate you to the next level.

2.  Local Tournaments
A division consists of various districts. Firstly, selection trials are held at district levels but in most cases, teams are selected on the basis of performance in the local tournament.

3.  District Level Tournaments
Selectors once again review the performance and if anyone from other districts have performed exceptionally well, they pick him up and draft into the divisional side which takes part in Inter-divisional tournaments.

4.  Inter-Divisional Tournaments
After Inter-divisional tournaments, the player who perform are picked up by selectors and are called up for a camp. In most states, they are selected through trials also but performance is the major criteria in selection. These selected players train under watchful eyes of state association coaches (mostly International players) and tour to various side tournaments before Ranji Trophy.

5.  Senior Divisions or Ranji Trophy
If you perform well-enough in above tournaments, your selection chances becomes high and in the end, selectors announce the Ranji Trophy team.

6.  Indian Cricket Team
If you perform very well. Once you start playing 1st division, it all depends upon you, if you play extraordinarily you can get selected for the state team / IPL teams and follow the same process for getting into the Indian team.



Football


Process for Selection in Football Team


1.  Join Small Club
If you are still in school, then you should probably be giving trials for the school team. If you get into your school team and you are playing tournaments, performing well then someone will spot your talent and offer you to play for some small time club.

2.  Local Tournaments
A division consists of various districts. Firstly, selection trials are held at district levels but in most cases, teams are selected on the basis of performance in the local tournament.

3.  District Level Tournaments
Then comes the district level football. You can play the district level tournaments with the club you are in. If you perform well in these tournaments and your team manages to win the tournament, you go to the next round i.e. the state level.

4.  Trial at S.A.I
After In India, we have various academies for every district and we have S.A.I (Sports authority of in India) in few cities. They conduct their trials every year. If you get selected to these academies after giving trials, they will make you a professional. You will be playing football every day and play in all the major tournaments.

5.  I-league Matches
After making your way through these academies by of course, performing well, and you do it consistently then the I-league coaches and scouts are always scouting players. If they spot you and approach you with a contract, you are in the top flight of the Indian football.

6.  National Team
By playing well here and by impressing scouts of Indian National Team, you can finally make your way through to the National team.

Hockey


Selection Procedure of Teams for Tournament Participation


1.  Players in camps / core probables are each considered for selection for national duty.

2.  The dates of the meeting / trials are informed to Sports Authority of India and the players in the camp / core probable at-least one week in advance along with the names of the Members of the Selection Committee.

3.  Selection Trials / Selection Meeting will access each of the available campers / core probable on the below criteria for considering in the team.
- Number of players eligible in the team for the tournament
- Number of positions of players in the team
- Balance in the different positions, defenders, free man and markers, central defenders and wide defenders, attacking and controlling midfielders, contact strikers and goalscoring and “pc-strikers”.
- Physical ability of the players
- Ability to translate the tactical plan of the team to on field performance.
- Players positive contribution in team dynamics
- Players experience in earlier matches and tournaments

4.  In addition to players selected by the Team, standby players – in case of injuries will also be selected.

5.  The final minutes of the Selection Committee Meeting during the trials / meeting is forwarded to Sports Authority of India within 48 hours via email. A copy of the Original Minutes of the Meeting is also provided to Sports Authority of India.
