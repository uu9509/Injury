Bhiwani Boxing Club

Bhiwani,Haryana

Established: : 2002                               
Sports : Boxing                                            
Chairman : Dr.kalpesh tanwar
Contact Us : +918796254398         

Details

Bhiwani boxing Club society of fitness is an Institute, remains a lab for all kinds of activities to develop all-round personality in students. None of which is possible without a sense of discipline which is grained into the spirits of the children. Disorderly habit of living open prove fatal. Therefore our institute emphasis always on self discipline. Today our Nation is in more need of disciplined and dedicated youth. Our Institution is giving proper direction to their surge of energy so that this country can reach a state of prosperity as never before.
On 17th February 2003, the meeting was held to constitute a Boxing academy in Bhiwani Distt. in the memory of Late Sh. Rajinder Singh yadav, Boxing Coach Bhiwani. Some upcoming Boxers of Bhiwani district think that without club or institution a discipline in boxing can not excel. In respect of this the youth boxer framed a boxing development body that is Regd. on 21st March 2003 under the society Act 1860. Regd. No. 3037 Sh. Rajiv Dalal I.P.S. (D.G.P.) President Haryana State Boxing Association provided direct affiliation to the boxing club who creates a new history in the field of Boxing for the development of Boxing game. Our Boxing club and fitness society was the first club which was affiliated by Haryana State Boxing Association. That is why the prominent Boxer are taking part directly in the state Championships. Sh. Jagdish Singh (Dronacharya Awardee) Boxing Coach is the Founder/President of the club.

Motilal Nehru School of Sports

Sonipat,Haryana

Established: : 2007                               
Sports : Cricket                                            
Chairman : Dr.Dinesh chandora
Contact Us : +918796254396         

Details

The Motilal Nehru School of Sports, Rai was founded in July 1973 by the Government of Haryana with the prime objective to provide excellent education facilities with extra emphasis on sports to deserving students. The school is organized on the Public School pattern. It is fully residential & co-educational. The School is a member of the Indian Public Schools Conference. The School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education for the Secondary (Class X) and Senior Secondary (Class XII) Examinations. Our endeavour is to help and guide the students in their vigorous pursuit of excellence in academics and sports.
The School is located at Rai, District Sonipat. Rai is popularly known as “Beeswan Meel” and is about 37 Km north of Delhi on the Sher Shah Suri Marg (GT Road). There are frequent bus services from Delhi’s Maharana Partap Inter State Bus Terminus, Kashmiri Gate, Rai “Beeswan Meel”. Buses coming from Chandigarh and Punjab will drop passengers on request at Beeswan

National Academy of Sports Management

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Established: : 2000                                
Sports : Cricket, Football                              
Chairman : MR. Robin Singh
Contact Us : +912228444111         

Details

NASM, National Academy of Sports Management, is an institute dedicated to producing highly professional individuals in the field of Sports Management. With the focused aim of creating professionally, academically and ethically sound sports managers and executives, NASM contributes to this burgeoning space in India.NASM is the first institute in India to offer University recognized Degree and Diploma courses in Sports Management. Identifying the need for experts in the industry, NASM instills its students with basic and advanced theories and techniques in the discipline of management, marketing, merchandising, tourism, law etc. related to the field of sports.
