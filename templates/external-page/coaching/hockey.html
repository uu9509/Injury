How to do the Spin

The spin can be used as an elimination skill or in a short corner. Get the ball in front of your body. Get the ball in the hook of your hockey stick. Give a big step forward with your left foot. Bring your right foot back past your left foot, so you can start the spin. Spin around and keep the ball under control and in the hook of your stick.

Ball Control

Don't worry about strength! You can have ripped forearms, a core, and huge arms, but that won't do anything for your shot if you're a beginner. First work on shooting. 100 shots a day will do wonders for you. We could recommend would be to get a shooting pad and work on pulling the puck towards your body.
