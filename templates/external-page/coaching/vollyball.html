Passing FUNDAMENTALS - How to PASS Volleyball.

One of the first things that you'll need to learn after getting your serve developed is how to pass the ball effectively to a player in the setting position and give your team the best chance of scoring a point.

The Rules of Volleyball

Vollyball is a team sport. Two teams of Six players each try to score, through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground. The game is played on a rectangular floor called the court, and there is a hoop at each end.

Extreme Vollyball Trick Shots

While many top players have perfected the jump serve, a simple overhand serve may be just as effective. Stand behind the serving line, toss the ball to a comfortable height over your head and hit it with a flat palm over the net as hard as you can.

Improve Spiking TIMING

Timing your spiking approach with a setter is very difficult and this video provides some tips on building a better spiking connection with your setter. The intention of a good set is to give others players an opportunity to score for the team.
